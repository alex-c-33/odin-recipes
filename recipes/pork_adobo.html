Pork adobo

Freshly cooked pork adobo

A very tasty dish from The Philippines. Pork adobo is made by marinating meat in a mixture of soy sauce, vinegar and garlic. This gives the dish it's unique flavour.

Listed below are the necessary ingredients and steps that need to be followed to create the dish.

ingridents

  • 866g of pork shoulder
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 teaspoons of black peppercorns
  • 2 teaspoons of sugar
  • ¼ cup of white vinegar
  • ⅓ cup of soy sauce
  • 2 cups of water

    • Steps

    1. In a medium pot over medium-high heat, add some oil and sear the pork until browned on all sides.
    2. Add the vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, bay leaf, peppercorns (tied in cheese cloth if desired), sugar, and water, and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat to medium low, cover, and simmer for 1 hour.
    3. Remove the cover and continue simmering for another 30 minutes to reduce the sauce.
    4. Serve and enjoy!
