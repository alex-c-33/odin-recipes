Chicken shawarma

Chicken shawarma ready to eat

Originating in the middle east, chicken shawarma can be enjoyed with rice or in bread wraps.

The steps and ingredients needed to make chicken shawarmas can be found below:

ingridents for the chicken shawarma

  • 800g of chicken thighs
  • Wraps
  • 1 chopped lettuce
  • 6-8 chopped tomatoes
    • ingredients for the marinade
  • 1 teaspoon of coriander
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of cardamon
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of cayenne
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of crushed garlic
  • juice of 1 lemon
    • ingredients for a lemon yogurt sauce
  • 250g of plain yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of crushed garlic
  • juice of 1/2 a lemon
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of pepper

    • Steps

    1. Mix all of the marinade ingredients into a large bowl or ziplock bag. Then add the chicken and leave to marinade in the fridge for 24hours.
    2. Mix all of the yogurt ingredients into a bowl and place into the fridge.
    3. After the chicken has finished marinating, remove it from the fridge and place the chicken onto a BBQ or stove and cook the chicken.
    4. Remove the chicken from the heat and leave it to stand for 3 minutes before cutting the meat into bitesized piecies.
    5. On one wrap, place some lettuce, tomatoes and chicken. Then add some of the yogurt sauce and roll the wrap.
