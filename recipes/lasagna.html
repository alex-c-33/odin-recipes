Lasagna

Freshly baked lasagna

This is one of my all time favourite meals and is ideal for serving guests at social events or when meeting with the family. This dish can appear to be a little difficult to make so I have created this super simple recipe that can be followed to make this tatsy dish.

The ingridents and steps needed to make the super simple lasagna are listed below:

ingredients

  • 400g minced beef
  • 9 lasagna pasta sheets
  • 1 jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 900g of cottage cheese
  • 3 cups of grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of dried parsley
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1/2 cup of water

    • Steps

    1. In a large pan over medium heat brown the ground beef. Drain the grease. Add spaghetti sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
    2. In a large bowl, mix together the cottage cheese, 2 cups of the mozzarella cheese, eggs, half of the grated Parmesan cheese, dried parsley, salt and ground black pepper.
    3. To assemble, in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish evenly spread 3/4 cup of the sauce mixture. Cover with 3 uncooked lasagna noodles, 1 3/4 cup of the cheese mixture, and 1/4 cup sauce. Repeat layers once more: top with 3 noodles, remaining sauce, remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Add 1/2 cup water to the edges of the pan. Cover with aluminum foil.
    4. Bake in a preheated 175 degrees C (350 degree F) oven for 45 minutes. Uncover and bake for an additional 10 minutes. Let it stand for 10 minutes before serving.
